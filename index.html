How to Use Deen Chat

Your Serial ID

Every user gets a unique 4-digit Serial ID when they join. This is your identity in Deen Chat!

Your ID: #0000
  • Find your ID in the top-right corner (next to your name)
  • Share this ID with others so they can message you directly
  • Your ID never changes - it's saved on your device

Admin Support Chat

By default, you'll see the Admin Support chat in your sidebar. This is where you can:

  • Ask questions to the admin team
  • Get help with any issues
  • Share feedback or suggestions

Private Messaging

Want to chat directly with another user? Here's how:

  • Click the New Chat button in the header
  • Enter the other person's 4-digit Serial ID (e.g., 2462)
  • Start chatting! Your conversation is private between you two
  • All your private chats appear in the sidebar
üí° Tip: Ask your friend for their Serial ID. You can find yours at the top-right corner!

Other Features

  • Change Name: Click on your name badge (top-right) to update your display name
  • Dark Mode: Toggle between light and dark themes using the Dark/Light button
  • Chat History: All your messages are automatically saved

Privacy Note

Your private chats are between you and the other person. Admins cannot see private messages in their panel - they can only be viewed from the database.
